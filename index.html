ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Bio-Medical Engineering was established in 2019. The department has an enthusiastic team of highly skilled faculty members. Students can study and practise in laboratories with highly advanced technology such as the Biomedical Instrumentation lab, the Diagnostic and Therapeutic lab, the Pathology and Microbiology lab, the Biochemistry lab, the Human Physiology lab, and the Circuits lab.

ABOUT SYMPOSIYUM

"BIOMUTANTZ "is an eclectic mix of cultures on campus,each with its own language and convention, through an exclusive sequence of technical and non-technical events.
